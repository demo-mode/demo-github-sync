Onboarding & Adoption

As new users move through trialing, onboarding, and getting value from the product, behavioral analytics can uncover indicators of stickiness. To improve adoption, use Mode to track data on engagement metrics to understand utilization of purchased plans, features, and seats, such as:

  • Average Days in Onboarding
  • % of Accounts with High License Utilization
  • Accounts with Low License Utilization


Retention

Once customers are onboarded and using the product, data teams can use Mode to predict customer health, identify potential churn, and learn how different cohorts act over time. To improve retention, use Mode to track data on:

  • NPS
  • Health

NPS Feedback


Net Promoter Score: 

  • Detractors: respondents who gave a score between 0-6
  • Passives: respondents who gave a score between 7-8
  • Promoters: respondents who gave a score between 9-10
