Customer Health Scoring by Individual Sales Rep

Scoring:

Each attribute is assigned a risk score on a scale of 1 to 3, with 1 representing the lowest risk and 3 highest risk


Methodology:

Customer health scores are bucketed into three categories, Firm, Account and Product Usage Health each with its own health score determined by scores assigned to attributes within each category. Each of these three scores are normalized to each have the same weight in determining the overall health score. The sum of these three scores is the overall health score.


Firm

  1. Industry health
  2. Funding health
  3. Revenue health

Account

  1. Time as customer
  2. Product Feature usage
  3. CSM Assessment of Renewal Risk/ Customer Engagement

Product Usage

  1. DAU/MAU
  2. Seat penetration


Each of these three scores are normalized to each have the same weight in determining the overall health score. The sum of these three scores is the overall health score.
