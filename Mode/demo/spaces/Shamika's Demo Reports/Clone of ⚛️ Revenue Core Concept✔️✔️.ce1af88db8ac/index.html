This report provides a comprehensive overview of Revenue for a sample SaaS B2B company. The main table that powers these metrics contains daily snapshots of all active engagements in Salesforce, starting in 2014 and continuing to the present day. This table is unaggregated and rich in dimensions and measures, such as is_month_to_month, total_seats_used, and annualized_overages. This design empowers sales leadership to dig deeper into the data and discover their own insights without ever needing to touch code, thus freeing up analyst time to focus on more complex strategic and operational questions.
