This dashboard is meant to showcase the many capabilities and visualizations that Mode's new visual explorer has to offer.

Pivot Table

Helps you summarize data tabularly on multiple dimensions. Mode's in-memory data engine (Helix) can handle millions of rows of data, which can now be visualized in a pivot table (and exported to a CSV).


You can even add color to your pivot tables to show conditional formatting and density.

Faceted Combo

Faceted combo visuals allow you to display different types of data in different ways on the same graph.


You may display columns, lines, colors, and size all on the same graph.

Funnel

With the flexibility of this new visualization system, you can even create a cone shaped funnel chart.


The example to the right shows quantity of paper reams sold broken down by region in the funnel.

Faceting

Faceting generates small multiples, each showing a different subset of the data.


Small multiples are a powerful tool for exploratory data analysis: you can rapidly compare patterns in different parts of the data and see whether they are the same or different.


The example to the right shows quantity of paper reams sold by account manager broken down by region & quarter.

Dual Axes Combo

These are great for visualizing multiple dimensions or measures in one chart.


The example to the right shows the quantity of reams sold broken down by region and rating over time.
